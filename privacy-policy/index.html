1. Introduction

Welcome to CLCKT ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy and trust are paramount to us. CLCKT is a creative and interactive aura detector application. It matches the visual vibe of user-uploaded gallery photos with manual location descriptions and state descriptions to calculate aura points, provide detailed reviews of your energetic presence, and deliver tailored recommendations.

This Privacy Policy clarifies how your uploaded photos, textual descriptions, and general usage statistics are collected, processed, and protected within the CLCKT mobile environment. By using the CLCKT application, you consent to the data collection and usage practices described in this document.

2. Information We Process

CLCKT operates on user-driven input to run its aura evaluation scans. To generate your aura reports, we collect and process the following specific data:

  • Uploaded Gallery Photos: The specific image you select from your device's photo gallery. Our engine processes the visual composition, lighting, colors, and aesthetics of the photo to analyze its "vibe".
  • Manual Location Descriptions: Textual descriptions you type manually to provide spatial and environmental context (e.g., "sitting in a dim rainy cafe", "under a sunny garden tree").
  • State Descriptions: Custom details, descriptions, or mood parameters you input to contextualize your present focus and refine the aura scan.

3. Aura Engine Processing

We process your uploaded photos and text inputs to run calculations on our secure servers, providing you with the following interactive reports:

01

Aura Point Scoring

Evaluating color spectrums and textual descriptors to calculate distinct spiritual and aesthetic energy scores.

02

Detailed Presence Review

Analyzing how your photo's vibe aligns with your environmental description to synthesize a deep review of your current presence.

03

Actionable Suggestions

Delivering personalized guidelines, mood tips, and recommendations based on your calculated aura state.

All photos uploaded are processed strictly to calculate your current score. We do not use your photos to build facial recognition templates, nor do we share your personal media with external companies or advertisers.

4. Data Storage, Security, & Retention

The security of your uploaded media and personal description parameters is handled with maximum care:

  • In-Transit Encryption: All uploaded photos and text descriptions are transmitted over secure, SSL-encrypted HTTPS channels.
  • Device Isolation: Local logs, history, and app parameters are kept in local device storage using standard encryption modules (EncryptedSharedPreferences).
  • Photo Deletion Protocols: Uploaded photos are processed immediately on our secure cloud nodes. Once the aura points, review, and suggestions are calculated, the analyzed photo is automatically removed from transient active servers, ensuring we do not store massive image backlogs of our users.

5. Required Device Permissions

To support photo scanning and cloud-based aura reviews, the CLCKT application requests the following specific permissions declared in our Android Manifest:

Permission Identifier Application Usage Classification
android.permission.INTERNET Mandatory. Used to communicate securely with our cloud servers to upload your selected photo, send description inputs, and fetch your aura points and detailed suggestions. Core Functionality
Photo Library Selection / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Optional/Prompted. Grants the app permission to open your photo gallery, allowing you to select and crop a custom photo to run through the vibe-check analyzer. User Initiated

CLCKT does not scan, index, or index-read any files in your media storage other than the exact, individual photo file you explicitly choose to upload through the Android System Photo Picker dialog.
