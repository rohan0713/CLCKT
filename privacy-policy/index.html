1. Introduction

Welcome to CLCKT ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and security. CLCKT is a smart camera monitoring and video surveillance application designed to let you manage your local security feeds, monitor live videos, schedule recordings, capture snapshots, and receive email alert notifications seamlessly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data and configurations within the CLCKT Android application. By installing and using the app, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not install or use CLCKT.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. CLCKT acts primarily as a dashboard controller for your personal surveillance systems, and we limit the collection of your data as follows:

  • Account Credentials: If you use a Google account or standard login to access the system, we collect your email address, name, and basic profile information to authenticate your session.
  • Server and Camera Configurations: To hook into your smart camera streams, we store your configuration settings, server IP addresses, custom camera titles, recording schedules, and multi-select parameters.
  • User-Generated Snapshots & Bookmarks: Any local snapshots you capture (PNG/JPG frames) or recordings you bookmark are saved locally to your device storage or synced directly to your private remote surveillance servers, depending on your configuration.
  • Email Alerts Configurations: To deliver notifications when camera motions are triggered, we process and store the recipient email addresses and custom notification templates you set.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is exclusively used to operate, maintain, and provide the core features of the CLCKT application:

01

Stream Connection

Linking the app securely to your designated live video feeds and processing server metadata.

02

Notification Systems

Delivering motion detection and server offline warnings to the email lists configured by you.

03

Schedule Automation

Validating and running user-defined recording intervals, duration routines, and active periods.

Important Note: We NEVER sell, monetize, rent, or lease your account information, server IP setups, camera feeds, or captured frames to any third-party advertisers or external agencies.

4. Data Storage, Security, & Retention

Security is the cornerstone of CLCKT. We take industry-standard precautions to shield your data:

  • Device Encryption: Sensitive data such as session keys and remote authorization tokens are stored on your Android device using local secure encryption protocols (EncryptedSharedPreferences).
  • Transmission: All API requests, stream interactions, and remote configurations are conducted strictly over secured, industry-standard encrypted tunnels (HTTPS / WSS).
  • Local Media: Captured camera snapshots remain in your device's isolated storage directory unless explicitly exported, ensuring you maintain complete offline ownership of your media.

5. Android System Permissions

To enable the application functionality, CLCKT requires the following system permission:

Permission Purpose Classification
INTERNET Enables communication with your surveillance servers, fetching configurations, and live-streaming camera footage. Core / Mandatory

This permission is mandatory for the basic operation of the application. We do not secretly request background permissions (such as location or microphone) unless explicitly needed for a user-initiated action.

6. Third-Party Services

The application may communicate with trusted third-party SDKs to enhance experience and maintain stability:

  • Firebase Remote Config & Analytics: Used to dynamically adjust dashboard UI layouts, distribute feature announcements, and track non-personally identifiable diagnostic crashes. Refer to the Google Privacy Policy for more details.

7. Account & Data Deletion Rights

We respect your ownership of your data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and related configurations at any time.

To initiate a data deletion request, you can simply click the data deletion button or email us at support@clckt.aura.app with your registered email identifier. We will process your request and permanently remove all configurations, email settings, and session credentials from our servers within 7 business days.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any adjustments by updating this website page and, where appropriate, publishing a notice inside the CLCKT mobile application.
